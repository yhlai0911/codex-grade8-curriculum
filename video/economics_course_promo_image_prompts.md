# Economics Course Promo Image Prompts

這份是給課程宣傳片用的主題生圖清單，可當作：

- 片頭 hero image
- 片尾 CTA image
- 圖到圖過場的首尾禎
- 章節插入的參考圖

共通規格：

- 畫面比例：`16:9`
- 風格：`cinematic, premium academic promo, international finance, economics, dark blue and gold palette`
- 品質：`high detail, volumetric light, clean composition, realistic lighting`
- 避免：`text, watermark, logo clutter, distorted hands, duplicate objects, low-res artifacts`
- 留白：畫面左側或右側要保留標題與字幕空間

## 01 片頭主視覺

用途：開場前 `2-4` 秒，建立高級感與國際財務主題。

Prompt:

```text
A cinematic hero scene for an economics and international finance course promo, global financial city skyline at night, subtle world map hologram, exchange rate charts floating in the air, gold and deep navy color palette, premium academic atmosphere, elegant lighting, strong depth, clean composition, realistic, high detail, wide 16:9 frame, empty space on the left for title
```

## 02 供需與價格

用途：從生活情境切進經濟概念，適合第一段到第二段之間。

Prompt:

```text
A stylish modern drink stand in Taiwan, summer city atmosphere, product demand surge visualized as glowing upward data trails, supply and demand concept illustrated with cinematic realism, economics education promo style, premium blue and amber lighting, high detail, 16:9, leave space for overlays
```

## 03 通膨與利率

用途：第三段通膨、央行、利率政策的過場參考圖。

Prompt:

```text
A dramatic central bank inspired control room, inflation heatwave visualized through glowing price tags and rising red-orange financial indicators, interest rate decision atmosphere, serious but modern economics promo scene, cinematic, realistic, premium dark blue background, high detail, 16:9
```

## 04 國際貿易與比較優勢

用途：第四段片頭或圖卡插入。

Prompt:

```text
International trade visual metaphor, Taiwan technology branding on one side, Southeast Asian manufacturing and raw material logistics on the other, shipping containers, port cranes, glowing trade routes across Asia, economics and global business promo look, cinematic realism, premium navy and green palette, 16:9
```

## 05 PPP / IRP 銜接

用途：第五段做從基礎概念升級到正式國際財務工具的視覺橋接。

Prompt:

```text
An abstract cinematic gateway from basic economics into international finance models, floating symbols for purchasing power parity and interest rate parity, layered charts, currency signals, elegant academic sci-fi style, dark blue and violet palette, premium financial education visual, high detail, 16:9
```

## 06 世界經濟影響企業

用途：第六段與片尾總結。

Prompt:

```text
Global economy impacting real business decisions, multinational company boardroom overlooking world trade routes and macroeconomic dashboards, subtle currency and commodity movement visuals, cinematic premium education promo, realistic, deep navy and warm gold lighting, 16:9, clear composition
```

## 07 教授出場主視覺

用途：真人主持人出場前的輔助參考圖，可作為定格或轉場緩衝。

Prompt:

```text
A young Taiwanese economics professor in a premium academic promo setting, confident, modern, approachable, seated in a cinematic studio with finance charts and world map light projections, realistic photography style, elegant dark blue and warm gold lighting, 16:9
```

注意：

- 「台灣人」屬於身份資訊，若用生成模型，較適合描述成 `Taiwan-based professor style` 或 `Taiwan academic visual context`，不要只靠臉部特徵硬判國籍。
- 若要跟現在影片風格一致，建議固定同一組色系與鏡頭語言，不要每段都換完全不同美術風格。

## 建議接法

- 片頭：`01 -> 教授出場`
- 第二段前：`02`
- 第三段前：`03`
- 第四段前：`04`
- 第五段前：`05`
- 片尾：`06 -> 課程標題或報名 CTA`
